# Goals & Actions

Same structure as the `TAXONOMY` dict in `reflection-annotation-contextual.py`
(goal → its actions). Strings are verbatim so human codes line up with the LLM
output. Definitions/levels are layered on separately.

## Goal 1: Establish a Supportive and Professional Environment
1. Addressed family member by name.
2. Introduced him/herself by name and role.
3. Clearly stated the name of the deceased family member.
4. Sat down. (Body language/Eye contact)
5. Displayed professional attire/presence.
6. Handled interruptions in non-disruptive manner.
7. Conducted interaction in organized manner.

## Goal 2: Assess and Align Expectations
1. Ensured all important survivors were present.
2. Determined knowledge survivors possessed.
3. Involved me when discussing reason for visit.
4. Elicited patient perspective of health situation.

## Goal 3: Deliver the News
1. Provided appropriate opening statement (warning shot).
2. Accurately/succinctly chronicled events leading to death.
3. Used phrase 'dead' or 'died' (avoided euphemisms).
4. Avoided jargon or explained terms.

## Goal 4: Manage the Emotional Response
1. Paused to allow family to assimilate information.
2. Responded to cues with appropriate touch.
3. Emotional response did not interfere with communication.
4. Legitimized my emotions.
5. Reinforced positive behaviors.

## Goal 5: Ensure Understanding and Facilitate Closure
1. Offered viewing of the deceased.
2. Established availability to answer questions.
3. Encouraged questions/concerns.
4. Summarized the interview.
5. Checked for accuracy during interview.
6. Reviewed next step(s).
7. Verified patient's understanding.
